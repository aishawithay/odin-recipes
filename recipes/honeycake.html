Soft and fluffy honey cake

Description

This Soft and fluffy honey castella cake is MOIST, not to sweet and so quick and easy to make. Seriously, you'll be hooked once you make this. And one more thing... ITS ONLY 6 INGREDIENTS!

Ingredients
  • 4 medium sized eggs
  • 100 g sugar
  • 60 ml Milk
  • 40 g honey
  • 2 tbsp or 30 ml neutral cooking oil
  • 110 g all purpose flour
  • A loaf pan

Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 270 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Line your pan with baking paper.
  3. In a bowl, Add your milk, honey, cooking oil and microwave for 40 seconds.
  4. In a seperate bowl, Seperate 4 eggs into whites and yolks.
  5. Beat the egg whites on medium speed, gradually adding your sugar in thirds, whisking for 1 minute before adding more.
  6. After all the sugar is added, Whisk on high for another 3-4 minutes.
  7. Add your yolks into the whites and beat for another minute.
  8. Sift your flour into the eggs and fold (DONOT MIX!)
  9. Now pour your liquids into the egg and flour mixture and again FOLD.
  10. Pour the batter into the pan and run a toothpick or skewer accros the batter to pop access bubbles.
  11. Place in the oven and bake for 40-45 minutes.
  12. let it cool slight before removing from pan and let it rest for atleast 4 hours. I swear its worth it.
  13. Enjoy.
